Cancellation Option:
If you availed of the Cancellation Option when booking your tickets and you now wish to cancel or transfer your tickets to another date, please email your request to cancellations@rathwood.com. Please note that you cannot claim a refund on or transfer of part of your ticket i.e. only full ticket cancellations and transfers can be made for your full ticket.
Please state your Ticket reference number, the name your tickets were booked under, your reason for cancelling or transferring and a daytime contact number.
You must email your request to cancellations@rathwood.com before the cancellation date and time stated on your Ticket. Please note once you have submitted a valid cancellation email it cannot be withdrawn.
You will be offered an alternative date and time, if one is available and a refund of your tickets less charges if another date and time is not available.
If you submit a cancellation AFTER this date and time, you will NOT be entitled to any refund.
Note: No refunds will be made for:

  • (a) Cancellations where the Cancellation Option has not been purchased
  • (b) Cancellations after the Cancellation Date and Time printed on your ticket, where you have paid for the cancellation option
  • (c) Cancellations must only be submitted to the email address (cancellations@rathwood.com) or they will be considered invalid and no refund will be made.
  • (d) Part of a ticket where only some of your group cannot attend. The Cancellation Option applies to the full ticket only.

Young Passengers: For safety reasons, children must be supervised and under the control of responsible adults at all times. Unaccompanied minors under 16 years will not be allowed to participate in Rathwood's Christmas Wonderland.

Passengers with Wheelchair requirements: Passengers who require the use of a wheelchair for their Christmas experience event must provide a suitable wheelchair themselves, which is compliant with all appropriate health and safety standards. They should notify Rathwood at info@rathwood.com or by telephoning 059 9156285 to request additional support if needed at least 48 hours prior to your event.
